# PoultryMitra Features

## 1. AI Conversational Assistant

Intelligent chatbot powered by Amazon Bedrock (Claude 3) that provides:
- Expert poultry farming advice
- Disease prevention guidance
- Feed optimization tips
- Best practices recommendations
- Context-aware responses using conversation history

## 2. Smart Disease Scanner

Upload poultry images for AI-powered disease detection:
- AWS Rekognition for image analysis
- Bedrock for disease identification
- Confidence scoring
- Treatment recommendations
- Historical tracking of detections

## 3. Farm Dashboard

Comprehensive analytics and monitoring:
- Total bird count tracking
- Active breed management
- Health status monitoring
- Revenue tracking
- Production trends visualization
- Recent activity feed

## 4. Breed Recommendation

AI-powered breed selection based on:
- Farming purpose (eggs/meat/dual)
- Climate conditions
- Experience level
- Budget constraints
- Detailed pros/cons analysis
- Production expectations

## 5. Business Planner

Financial planning and analysis:
- Investment calculation
- Break-even analysis
- ROI projections
- Monthly expense tracking
- Revenue forecasting
- AI-generated business insights

## 6. Invoice & Expense Manager

Complete financial tracking:
- Income recording
- Expense tracking
- Category management
- Transaction history
- Profit/loss calculation
- Visual summaries

## 7. Health Scheduler

Vaccination and health management:
- Vaccination schedule creation
- Due date reminders
- Status tracking (pending/completed/overdue)
- Bird count management
- Notes and documentation

## 8. Multi-Language Support

Accessible to diverse farmers:
- English
- Hindi (हिंदी)
- Telugu (తెలుగు)
- Easy language switching
- Localized UI elements
